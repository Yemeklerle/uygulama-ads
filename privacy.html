Privacy Policy for Crypto Screener

Last Updated: December 5, 2025

This Privacy Policy outlines the practices of Crypto Screener (the "App") regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our services.

1. Information Collection

The App does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers. We do not require user registration.

2. Local Storage & Caching

The App uses your device's local storage to temporarily cache market data (prices, volume, charts) retrieved from public APIs. This is done solely to improve performance and reduce data usage. This data is not transmitted to us or any third party.

3. Third-Party Services

The App displays data fetched from third-party services, specifically the CoinGecko API. By using the App, you also agree to be bound by CoinGecko's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

4. Advertising (AdMob)

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use cookies or unique device identifiers (Advertising ID) to serve personalized ads based on your interests. You can opt-out of personalized advertising in your Android device settings.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the developer contact information provided on the Google Play Store page.
